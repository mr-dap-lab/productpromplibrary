# 🚀 Product Prompt Library

A curated collection of AI prompts for product managers and product teams to accelerate backlog building, product discovery, and digital product development.

## What's inside

The **[PROMPT_LIBRARY.md](PROMPT_LIBRARY.md)** contains ready-to-use prompts organised into 12 categories:

| # | Category | What it helps you do |
|---|----------|----------------------|
| 1 | [User Stories & Backlog Items](PROMPT_LIBRARY.md#1-user-stories--backlog-items) | Generate, refine, and break down user stories |
| 2 | [Epics & Themes](PROMPT_LIBRARY.md#2-epics--themes) | Define epics from business goals and write hypothesis statements |
| 3 | [Backlog Prioritization](PROMPT_LIBRARY.md#3-backlog-prioritization) | Apply RICE, MoSCoW, and value/effort frameworks |
| 4 | [Product Discovery & User Research](PROMPT_LIBRARY.md#4-product-discovery--user-research) | Create interview guides, synthesise insights, build personas |
| 5 | [Problem Framing & Opportunity Assessment](PROMPT_LIBRARY.md#5-problem-framing--opportunity-assessment) | Write problem statements, size opportunities, apply JTBD |
| 6 | [Product Requirements Documents (PRD)](PROMPT_LIBRARY.md#6-product-requirements-documents-prd) | Generate and review full PRDs |
| 7 | [Feature Specifications](PROMPT_LIBRARY.md#7-feature-specifications) | Write feature specs, API requirements, and dev handoffs |
| 8 | [Roadmap Planning](PROMPT_LIBRARY.md#8-roadmap-planning) | Build quarterly roadmaps and Now/Next/Later plans |
| 9 | [Stakeholder Communication](PROMPT_LIBRARY.md#9-stakeholder-communication) | Draft updates, release announcements, and difficult conversations |
| 10 | [Metrics & Success Criteria](PROMPT_LIBRARY.md#10-metrics--success-criteria) | Define KPIs, OKRs, and A/B tests |
| 11 | [Digital Product Creation](PROMPT_LIBRARY.md#11-digital-product-creation) | Ideate products, write vision statements, define MVPs |
| 12 | [Competitive Analysis](PROMPT_LIBRARY.md#12-competitive-analysis) | Analyse competitors and write positioning statements |

## How to use

1. Open [PROMPT_LIBRARY.md](PROMPT_LIBRARY.md) and find the category relevant to your task.
2. Copy the prompt template.
3. Replace the `[bracketed placeholders]` with your specific context.
4. Paste into your preferred AI assistant (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Copilot, etc.).
5. Iterate on the output — add context, ask for revisions, or chain prompts together.
